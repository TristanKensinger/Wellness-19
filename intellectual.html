Intellectual

Humans have a intellectual need. We need to be constantly stimulated and challenged or we will not be fully satisfied in life. Especially in a pandemic where daily life starts to get routine and boring it is important to exercise the brain. Now is the perfect time to learn something new. Below we have a couple of things you could start learning today.

Learning a Language

A great way to spent all of your free time is learning a new language. When the quarentine lifts and air travel is back to normal im sure people are going to be traveling all over. Learning a new language can be very fun and rewarding. One very popular place people go to learn new languages is Duolingo. Here is a link. Duolingo

Learning to Type

Yes, it might be a weird thing to be doing at home during lockdown, but when the world is moving more and more to the digital age, knowing how to type fast can become an essential skill. There a tons of fun ways to learn things and typing is no exception. We have linked a website that hosts tons of free games that will help improve your typing. Typing Games

Journaling

Not only is journaling a great way to keep your brain active, it can also just improve your overall wellness in almost every category. Journaling can take pretty much any form. We suggest just grabbing some old notbook and writing whatever is on your mind. Any idea, feeling, or emotion can be written down in your journal. Journaling can also help your personal development, helping you think through difficult situations and planing your future.
