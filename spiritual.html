Spiritual

Spirituality is the quality of being concerned with the human spirit or soul as opposed to material or physical things. Spirituality is a truly individual experience which can mean varies from person to person. Whether you are or are not religious it is important to exercise the spiritual part of your being to stay happy and healthy. Below are a couple of different things you can do at home to exercise your spirituality.

The 555 Practice

The 555 practice is a morning mindfulness practice created by Uma Beepat that can put the mind in a state of spirituality. "It basically just involves spending five minutes in meditation, five minutes stretching, and five minutes mentally preparing for the day," explains Beepat, a consciousness coach and owner of Lotus Wellness Center. Lotus Wellness Center

Meditation

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state. Many people meditate to ground themselves and feel more connected to the present. Many people claim that meditation can boost one's spirituality and restore happiness and motivation. Mindful.org offers guidance on how to begin meditating. Mindful.org
