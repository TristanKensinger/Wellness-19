Physical

A great way to stay happy and healthy is to stay active. Many people have normal workout regimes like going to the gym, going out for a run, or playing sports. However, during this pandemic we are limited to the way we exercise. This is why we have linked some exercises you can do at home to stay healthy.

Cardio

Its been proven that doing cardio makes us feel happier. It can boost our confidence, happiness, and reduce stress. The only issue is finding a good place to go for a run or walk. We recommend giving Park Finder a try. All you have to do is type in very basic information of where you live and it shows you where the closest park is to you. Park Finder

Core

Core exercises train the muscles in your pelvis, lower back, hips and abdomen to work in harmony. This leads to better balance and stability, whether on the playing field or in daily activities.
