Social

Humans are social creatures so it is important that we all get our social fill on a daily basis. During a time like this our only real option of human to human contact is the people that we are quarentining with. Other than spending quality time with them like watching movies and going for walks, most of us turn to the internet for a solution. Here are some helpful resources to keep you social while socially distancing.

Netflix Party

We are all used to watching movies and shows with our friends. Although the pandemics hinders our abilities to watch movies in person with our friends, it sure doesn't stop us from watching movies remotely. Netflix party allows you to watch movies and shows with you friends at home. Netflix Party synchronizes video playback and adds group chat to your favorite Netflix shows. Netflix Party

Book Clubs

A great way to stay social and make friends is to bond over some of your favorite books. Bookship is social reading app that lets you share reading experiences across the city or planet like a virtual book club. Bookship

Video Games

Video games are a great way to stay social with friends and family. Its important to note that not all video games are violent and for teenagers.
